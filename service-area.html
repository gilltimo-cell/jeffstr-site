Serving All of Summit County

Summit County is our primary service territory. Whether you're in urban Akron or a rural township on the county's edge, we can get to you. We bring the same crew, the same equipment, and the same standards to every job — no matter where in the county you are.

We also take jobs outside Summit County on a case-by-case basis, particularly for crane-assisted removals, large commercial jobs, and referrals from existing customers. Call and tell us what you've got — if we can help, we will.

Why Local Matters

When you hire a locally-owned tree service like Jeff's Tree Removal, you're getting a crew that knows the region. We know the tree species common in Northeast Ohio — the massive silver maples, the hundreds of ash trees dying from the emerald ash borer, the old oaks in historic neighborhoods, the evergreens planted too close to houses in the 1970s. We know the soil, the weather patterns, and what trees look like after an Akron winter.

That local knowledge matters. It affects how we assess jobs, how we bid them, and how we execute. We're not a franchise that dropped into Summit County last year. We're here, we're invested, and we care about our reputation in these communities.
