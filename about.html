Who We Are

Jeff's Tree Removal is a family-owned tree service based in Akron, Ohio. We serve homeowners and property owners throughout Summit County and the surrounding Northeast Ohio region — providing professional tree removal, trimming, stump grinding, and crane-assisted services for jobs of every size.

We started this business the right way: with proper licensing, full insurance coverage, real equipment, and a commitment to doing the job correctly the first time. That foundation hasn't changed. Every crew member on every job understands that we're working on someone's home — and that demands care, precision, and respect for the property.

Rooted in Akron

Jeff lives and works in Akron. When you call us, you're talking to someone who knows Summit County — the neighborhoods, the tree species, the soil conditions, the seasonal challenges. That local knowledge shows in the quality of every estimate and every cut.

We've worked across Akron's neighborhoods, out through Cuyahoga Falls and Stow, into Hudson and Kent, and across the county line into Northeast Ohio communities. The region's mix of mature oaks, silver maples, ash (many now dead or dying from the emerald ash borer), and storm-prone silver maples keeps us busy year-round — and we know exactly how to handle each species and situation.

What Makes Us Different

There are a lot of tree services operating in the Akron area. Some are legitimate, licensed operations. Others are not — and in tree work, the difference matters. A crew without insurance working on your property creates real liability exposure for you if something goes wrong.

Here's what sets Jeff's Tree Removal apart from the alternatives:

🏗️

We Own a Knuckle Boom Crane

This is our biggest differentiator. Our crane gives us access to large, complex trees that other crews simply can't handle safely. Trees near houses, power lines, fences, or in confined spaces — our crane changes what's possible. Most tree services in the area don't have one, which means they either turn those jobs down or outsource them. We handle them ourselves.

🔒

Fully Licensed & Insured

We carry full general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We don't cut corners on this. If something unexpected happens on your property, you're protected — and so is our crew. Ask for proof of insurance before hiring any tree service. We'll provide it without hesitation.

📋

Honest, Upfront Estimates

We walk every job before quoting it. We look at the tree, the access, the surroundings, and anything else that affects how the work gets done. Then we give you a clear price before any work starts. No hidden fees, no scope creep, no surprise invoices after the job is done.

🧹

Complete Cleanup, Every Time

We chip, haul, and clean up all debris before we leave. Your yard will be cleaner after we're done than when we started. We don't leave stumps unless you ask us to, and we don't leave brush piles for you to deal with later.

🤝

A Reputation We Care About

When your business is local, your reputation is everything. We work in the same communities our customers live in. We see our customers at the grocery store, at church, at their kids' games. That accountability drives us to do every job right — not just the ones where someone's watching closely.

Our Commitment to Safety

Tree work is genuinely dangerous. Every job involves working at height, around heavy equipment, near structures, and often under pressure from weather or urgent circumstances. We take safety seriously — not as a marketing talking point, but because we want our crew to go home safely at the end of every day, and we want your property undamaged when we leave.

We follow industry-standard safety practices on every job. We assess each tree and removal situation before work begins. We use proper rigging, proper PPE, and proper equipment for every task. When a situation requires a crane instead of a climber, we use the crane. Safety isn't negotiable.

Ready to Work With Us?

Whether you have a hazardous tree that needs to come down today or you're planning a property cleanup this spring, we're ready to help. Call us for a free estimate — we'll come out, walk the job with you, and give you a clear picture of what the work entails and what it'll cost.
