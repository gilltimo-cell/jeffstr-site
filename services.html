Tree Removal

Dead, diseased, damaged, or dangerously positioned trees need to come down — and how they come down matters. We assess every removal job before we start, plan the safest approach, and execute it with the right equipment. We handle trees of all sizes, from small ornamentals to 100-foot hardwoods, and we leave your property clean when we're done.

Whether it's a single dead maple in an open backyard or a large oak leaning toward your house, we bring the same methodical, safety-first approach to every job.

  • Safe removal of dead, diseased, or hazardous trees
  • Crane-assisted removal for large or difficult trees
  • Complete cleanup — chipping, hauling, and debris removal
  • Stump grinding available at the same time (ask when scheduling)

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Proper trimming isn't just about appearance — it's about the long-term health and structure of the tree. We trim to industry standards, removing dead wood, crossed branches, and growth that creates structural weakness or storm risk. We don't top trees, and we don't make cuts that damage the tree's long-term health.

Trimming also improves clearance around your home, reduces weight on limbs over your roof or driveway, and keeps trees looking their best through the seasons.

  • Crown thinning, cleaning, and raising
  • Deadwood removal to reduce storm risk
  • Clearance trimming for structures and power lines
  • Fruit tree and ornamental pruning

Knuckle Boom Crane Service

Our knuckle boom crane is our biggest differentiator in the Akron market. Most tree services don't own or operate crane equipment — when they face a complex removal near a house or power lines, they either turn it down or subcontract it. We handle it ourselves.

The crane's articulating arm can reach over fences, rooflines, and obstacles to lift and lower large tree sections precisely — no drops, no damage to surrounding structures. If you have a large or difficult tree that other crews have been hesitant to touch, call us.

  • Large trees near homes, fences, and structures
  • Trees near or entangled with power lines
  • Confined spaces with no ground access
  • Structurally compromised trees too risky to climb

Stump Grinding & Removal

After a tree is removed, the stump is often more trouble than it's worth — it attracts insects, creates a tripping hazard, and makes mowing an obstacle course. Our stump grinding equipment grinds stumps below grade so the area can be replanted, seeded, or built over.

We grind stumps of all sizes, including large multi-root stumps and stumps in tight spots. The resulting wood chips can be used as mulch or hauled away — your choice.

  • Grinding to 6–12 inches below grade
  • All stump sizes including large hardwoods
  • Chips left on site (usable as mulch) or hauled away
  • Can be scheduled same-day as tree removal
