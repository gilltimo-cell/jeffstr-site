## SEO Requirements (from audit)
- Title tags must include city + keyword (e.g., "Tree Removal Akron OH | Jeff's Tree Removal")
- H1 tags optimized (no stray colons, include location + keyword)
- Meta descriptions on every page (~155 chars, include phone + keyword + city)
- "Akron" and surrounding cities mentioned naturally throughout body copy

- FAQ schema markup on faq.html
